Founding Director

Mr D Mokaleng is the founding member of MOKALENG IT, an IT Company offering a range of services, including web design, networking support, IT training, and consultation. The company emphasize skills development through certifications like MICT-SETA and Microsoft qualifications. They provide customized IT solutions to various clients, including law firms, municipalities, corporate businesses, and the public, covering domains like hardware, software, server management, and ICT training.

Accreditation Background

MICT SETA Accreditation (ACC/2021/02/1345) 2021

This accreditation signifies that MOKALENG IT complies with the rigorous quality standards set by SAQA and QCTO for education and training. It ensures that the learning programs offered are of high quality, industry-relevant, and meet national standards, enhancing learners' qualifications and employability in the tech sector.
